## 2. Data Collection

## 3. Data Storage

ZeroDM uses the **Chrome `storage` API** to store a small amount of **non-sensitive data locally on your device**, such as:

No LinkedIn messages, usernames, passwords, or personal content are stored.
All stored data remains **local to your browser only** and is **never transmitted externally**.
